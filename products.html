Crushing, Screening & Washing Equipment

High-quality equipment for mining, construction, and recycling industries

Crushers Mobile Crushers Feeders Screens Recycling Equipment Conveyors Spare Parts

Crushers

Jaw Crushers

Primary crushing of hard materials like granite, basalt, and limestone. Available in small (75-100 TPH), medium (100-250 TPH), and large (250-800 TPH) sizes.

Cone Crushers

Secondary and tertiary crushing of medium-hard materials. Available in small (75-200 TPH), medium (200-500 TPH), and large (500-1000 TPH) sizes.

Impact Crushers

Versatile crushing solution for softer materials. Available in small (50-150 TPH), medium (150-300 TPH), and large (300-500 TPH) sizes.

Roller Crushers

Secondary and tertiary crushing of medium-hard materials like limestone, coal, and slag. Available in small (5-10 TPH), medium (15-30 TPH), and large (30-100 TPH) sizes.

Mobile Crushers

Mobile Jaw Crusher

Portable crushing solution for construction and demolition. Available in small (50-150 TPH), medium (150-300 TPH), and large (300-800 TPH) sizes.

Feeders

Grizzly Feeders

Pre-screening and feeding large material to crushers. Available in small (25-50 TPH), medium (50-200 TPH), and large (200-500 TPH) sizes.

Vibrating Feeders

Reliable feeding solution for crushing and screening plants with adjustable feed rates.

Screens

Vibrating Screens

Material segregation by size. Available in small (2-3 decks, up to 50 TPH), medium (4-5 decks, 50-150 TPH), and large (6+ decks, 150-600 TPH) configurations.

Recycling Equipment

C&D Recycling Plants

Construction and Demolition waste recycling. Available in small (10-25 TPH), medium (25-100 TPH), and large (100-400 TPH) configurations.

Conveyors

Belt Conveyors

Used to transport crushed materials between crushers and screens. Available in various sizes and capacities.

Stacker Conveyors

For creating stockpiles of processed materials with adjustable heights and positions.

Spare Parts

Crusher Spare Parts

Jaw plates, cheek plates, toggle plates, mantles, bowl liners, blow bars, and more for all crusher types.

Screen Spare Parts

Screen mesh, springs, bearings, feeders, decks, and eccentric shafts for all screen types.

Conveyor Components

Belts, rollers, idlers, pulleys, and drive components for all conveyor systems.
